Riverside.

Cafe and Roastery

Uzhhorod is the city of coffee. Coffee tastes even better when it's freshly roasted in coffee houses. And it is exactly what we do!

We roast your favorite kind - Arabica, Robusta, Liberica, Excelsa. We also prepare it exactly as you love it - classic, cappuccino and latte (ordinary, haselnut or coconut milk), kalita, chemex, aeropress, cold-brew. You name it - we make it!

We also provide you with tasty breakfasts, brunchs and lunchs. Sweet or sour - just have a look at our menu.

So next time you are passing by - don't be shy and pay as a visit.
